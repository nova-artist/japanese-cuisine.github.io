Savoury

Japanese food or Washoku (和食) centres around glutinous rice, miso soup, vegetables and usually fish. However, Japanese food can be quite diverse and unique with dishes you'd never expect. Japan is typically well known for its Sushi, but a personal favourite of mine is Salmon Teriyaki, which is soy, garlic and ginger marinated grilled salmon, is usually served with glutinous rice and steamed vegetables such as edamame, broccoli, peas etc. Prominent Japanese flavours include ginger, soy, Miso (soybean paste), Shichimi (Japanese seven-spice), Yuzu (a Japanese hybrid citrus fruit) and sesame.
