Savoury

Japanese food or Washoku (和食) centres around glutinous rice, miso soup, vegetables and usually fish. However, Japanese food can be quite diverse and unique with dishes you'd never expect.

Wagashi

Wagashi (和菓子) are traditional Japanese sweets often served with green tea, and are made in a variety of shapes, sizes, colours, flavours and textures.

Japan

Japan (日本) is an island in East Asia comprised of five main islands; Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, Kyushu and Okinawa. Tokyo is the country's capital.
