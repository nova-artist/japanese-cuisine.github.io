About Japan

Japan is a beautiful island country renowned for its cuisine, art, music, rich culture and fascinating history. Japan is also well known for its animation industry which makes an amazing $19 billion in revenue a year, and has over 430 production studios with world famous names such as Studio Ghibli and Toei Animation. Japan really is a unique country and has many abstract customs considered unusual to westerners, for example, it is customary for Japanese people to sleep on the job and is seen as proof that you are hard working. Another aspect of Japanese culture that I find both commendable and intruiging is the fact that there are very few, if any Janitors in Japan! This is supposedly because the streets in Japan are very clean and have next to no litter, the reason being that Japanese people rarely litter and have a very clean mentality. There are also many volunteers, of all ages that clean the streets of Japan because they want to live in such an environment.
